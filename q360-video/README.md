# Q360 — Product Video Package (≈5 minutes)

A production-ready package for a cinematic, B2B product film for **Q360** by
Solutions360 — the ERP / PSA platform purpose-built for technology integrators.

**Reference / style model:** *"A smarter, faster way to build AI-ready data
centers | Vertiv™ OneCore"* (YouTube `TXRAZ16HVHY`). The goal is the same
feel — calm, confident voiceover; smooth motion graphics and clean product
shots; premium and *realistic*, never over-hyped.

> ⚠️ This package is the blueprint for the film (script, storyboard, timed
> voiceover, on-screen text, and direction). It is **not** a rendered MP4.
> Hand it to an editor / motion team, or feed the voiceover script into a TTS
> tool and the storyboard into an AI video generator to assemble the cut.

## What's inside

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `01-voiceover-script.md` | Clean, timed English VO — ready for a voice actor or TTS. |
| `02-storyboard.md` | Scene-by-scene shot list: visuals, on-screen text, transitions, timing. |
| `03-production-notes.md` | Voice direction, music, pacing, brand/style guide, and how to assemble. |

## Specs at a glance

- **Length:** ~5:00 (300s), 7 acts / 16 scenes
- **Narration:** English, single calm authoritative voice (see direction notes)
- **Aspect:** 16:9, 1080p/4K master; 1:1 and 9:16 cutdowns suggested
- **Tone:** premium, grounded, "smooth and realistic, not over" (per brief)
