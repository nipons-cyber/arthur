# Q360 — Storyboard & Shot List

16 scenes across 7 acts. Each scene lists the **visual**, **on-screen text**
(keep it minimal — let the VO lead), **transition**, and **duration**.

Visual language (carried throughout): clean, airy compositions; slow,
purposeful camera moves (push-ins, gentle parallax); cool neutral palette with
a single brand-accent color; soft real-world footage blended with elegant UI
motion graphics. **No fast cuts, no flashing, no hype.** Smoothness comes from
long holds, eased motion, and matched cuts on action.

> Tip for an AI video generator: prompt for "cinematic, slow dolly, shallow
> depth of field, soft natural light, muted color grade, 24fps" and keep each
> generated clip 4–8s, then assemble per the timings below.

---

## ACT 1 — The world of the integrator (0:00–0:35)

**S1 · 0:00–0:12** — *Open on scale & craft.*
Slow aerial drift over a city at dawn; cut to a technician's hands terminating
cable; a stadium's lights powering on.
On-screen: *(none — or faint timestamped vignettes)*
Transition: slow cross-dissolves. Music: single sustained pad, building.

**S2 · 0:12–0:35** — *The breadth of the work.*
Quick, calm montage: hospital corridor with security cameras, a control room,
an integrator's van pulling up to a site at sunrise.
On-screen: *The work is complex.* (small, lower-third, fades)
Transition: match-cut on motion into Act 2.

---

## ACT 2 — The hidden cost of growth (0:35–1:10)

**S3 · 0:35–0:52** — *Fragmentation, visualized.*
Abstract UI motion: four disconnected "islands" (Quotes, Projects, Inventory,
Accounting) floating apart, thin broken lines between them.
On-screen: labels on each island.
Transition: the broken lines flicker out.

**S4 · 0:52–1:10** — *The cost.*
A profit/margin line on a dashboard quietly sliding down; a clock; a re-keyed
spreadsheet cell. Desaturated, slightly tense.
On-screen: *Every disconnect is a delay.*
Transition: screen darkens to near-black before Act 3 — the "reset" beat.

---

## ACT 3 — Introducing Q360 (1:10–1:45)

**S5 · 1:10–1:25** — *The turn.*
From black, a single point of light; the four islands glide back together and
snap into one clean unified interface. Color returns to the grade.
On-screen: **Q360** logo resolves, centered.
Transition: confident, eased scale-in. Music: warm theme enters.

**S6 · 1:25–1:45** — *Positioning.*
Hero shot of the Q360 dashboard on a laptop in a real office; rack focus to a
person nodding.
On-screen: *One platform. Built by integrators, for integrators.*
Transition: push-in into the UI to begin the product walk.

---

## ACT 4 — How it works: one lifecycle (1:45–3:05)

*Treat this as one continuous, flowing journey — each module hands off to the
next with a connecting line/particle so the "one source of truth" idea is felt.*

**S7 · 1:45–2:05** — *Sales & proposals.*
UI: a proposal builds itself — line items, dynamic labour rates, a margin
indicator ticking into the green.
On-screen: *Sales & Proposals* · *Dynamic labour rates*

**S8 · 2:05–2:25** — *Project management & job costing.*
The won proposal animates into a Gantt/schedule; job-cost figures populate live.
On-screen: *Project Management · Job Costing* · *Real-time, no re-entry*

**S9 · 2:25–2:45** — *Service & dispatch (mobile).*
Cut to the field: a technician's phone showing the work order; a dispatch map
with a tech icon moving along a route.
On-screen: *Service & Dispatch* · *In the palm of their hand*

**S10 · 2:45–2:58** — *Inventory & fulfillment.*
Warehouse shelf; UI overlays stock levels syncing; a parts pick-list checks off.
On-screen: *Inventory & Fulfillment*

**S11 · 2:58–3:05** — *Full accounting.*
All prior modules' lines converge into a single ledger/accounting view.
On-screen: *Full, built-in accounting · One source of truth*
Transition: pull back to reveal the whole connected system glowing softly.

---

## ACT 5 — The payoff (3:05–3:55)

**S12 · 3:05–3:28** — *Real-time profitability.*
A clean executive dashboard; a profitability gauge on a live job moving into
the green; a subtle "today" marker (acting while it matters).
On-screen: *See profit while you can still act on it.*

**S13 · 3:28–3:55** — *Scale without chaos + any device.*
Confident montage: more pins appearing on a map (locations), more techs, a
calm ops floor. End on the same dashboard mirrored across desktop, web, phone.
On-screen: *Scale without the chaos.* → *Desktop · Web · Mobile*
Transition: devices align, dissolve to people.

---

## ACT 6 — Proof (3:55–4:30)

**S14 · 3:55–4:30** — *Credibility.*
Real faces / real sites montage (or tasteful stat cards if no footage):
counters animate up.
On-screen (sequential): *500+ integrators* → *AV · Security · Fire · Life
Safety* → *25 to 2,000+ employees* → **Built by integrators. For integrators.**
Transition: settle, then a calm beat of silence before the close.

---

## ACT 7 — Brand close (4:30–5:00)

**S15 · 4:30–4:50** — *The thesis.*
Slow hero push-in on Q360 running, then a wide of the team at work.
On-screen: *Your business does remarkable work. Your software should keep up.*

**S16 · 4:50–5:00** — *Logo & CTA.*
Clean end card on brand background.
On-screen: **Q360** · *The last business management platform you'll ever need.*
· Solutions360 logo · website URL · **Book a demo**
Music: resolve to a single sustained note; hold 3s; fade.
